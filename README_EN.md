# Zhongyuan Bangban — Full-Chain Business Assistance & Agency System

**Tagline:** Human Experts + AI Agents · Full-Lifecycle Business Assistance — Let Enterprises Focus on Core Business.

Zhongyuan Bangban is a one-stop business assistance and agency service system that combines a doctoral-level expert team with customized AI agents. It covers the entire lifecycle of enterprises and individuals, from government affairs handling to technical consulting, from standardization services to AI-powered tooling.

---

## What We Do

We help enterprises and individuals in three integrated ways:

### 1. Government Affairs Assistance & Agency
**Powered by government service platforms**

We assist individuals and enterprises throughout their full lifecycle — business registration, licensing, qualification handling, policy matching, subsidy applications, and more. We handle window visits, form filling, document submission, and progress tracking, so clients save time and avoid hassle.

### 2. Technical Consulting & Escort Services
**Led by a PhD-level standardization team**

We provide end-to-end technical consulting: standardization talent training, professional certification tutoring, project application support, standard drafting, and pilot-project applications. Our doctoral team accompanies clients through every key milestone.

### 3. AI Agent Services
**Industry-customized AI tools**

We offer 12 specialized AI agents tailored to different industries and business scenarios. AI handles 80% of standardized work, while human experts step in at the 20% of critical decision points.
